FIFO (First-In-First-Out)

Replaces the oldest page in memory. Simple but can lead to poor performance in some cases.

LRU (Least Recently Used)

Replaces the page that hasn't been used for the longest time. More accurate but requires tracking usage.

Optimal

Replaces the page that will not be used for the longest time in the future. Best case but not practical in real systems.
